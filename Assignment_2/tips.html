Tips to Capture best Photos:

  • Use the rule of thirds:

    Align your subject along the grid lines for a balanced and visually pleasing composition.

  • Mind the lighting:

    Natural light is your best friend. Shoot during golden hour or seek out soft, diffused light for the best results.

  • Focus on detail:

    Don’t be afraid to capture close-up shots. Textures, patterns, and small details can add depth and interest to your photos.

  • Stablize your camera:

    Use a tripod or steady your hands to avoid blurry images, especially in low-light situations.

  • Keep your lens clean:

    A dirty lens can ruin your shot. Make sure it’s clean for clear and sharp images.

  • Avoid direct flash:

    Flash can create harsh shadows. Use natural light or indirect flash to soften the look of your photos.
